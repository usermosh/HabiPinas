Meet the Hands Behind the Weave

At HabiPinas, every product begins with local hands and local stories. We work with selected Filipino producers, small businesses, cooperatives, and artisan communities to bring woven products closer to online consumers.

By featuring product origins, province labels, and artisan or producer details, HabiPinas promotes appreciation for Filipino craftsmanship while helping local products gain wider digital exposure.

Our Purpose

HabiPinas was created to make Philippine weaving products more accessible to modern consumers while supporting the talent and craftsmanship of local producers from different provinces.

What We Support

Direct sourcing from local producers
Proper product origin crediting
Digital exposure for Filipino weaving products
Appreciation of province-based craftsmanship
Ethical and respectful product storytelling

Ethical Sourcing Statement

HabiPinas does not copy ethnic patterns or claim cultural designs without proper sourcing. Products will be sourced from legitimate producers, small businesses, cooperatives, or artisan partners. If exact artisan names or photos are used, permission will be requested before publishing.

Weaving Traditions We Feature

Each HabiPinas collection is connected to a specific weaving tradition and province.

Every woven product has a story. HabiPinas helps carry that story forward.
