Our Story

How HabiPinas came to be — and why Filipino weaving matters.

HabiPinas was created from the idea that Filipino weaving deserves a stronger place in the digital market. Many woven products from the Philippines are meaningful, artistic, and useful, but they are often limited to regional markets, tourist areas, or small online pages.

Our business aims to connect these local products to modern online consumers through an organized e-commerce platform. By using a website and Shopee store, HabiPinas makes it easier for customers to discover, understand, and purchase woven accessories from selected provinces.

We believe that every product should not only be beautiful, but also meaningful. That is why each HabiPinas product page includes its province, source category, material, care instructions, and producer story.

Mission

To make Philippine woven accessories more accessible through e-commerce while supporting local producers, cultural appreciation, and meaningful Filipino craftsmanship.

Vision

To become a recognized online platform for curated Philippine woven accessories that connects customers to local producers and province-based weaving stories.
