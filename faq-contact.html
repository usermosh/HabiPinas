Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question about our products, orders, or delivery? Find quick answers below.

Are the products locally made?

Yes. HabiPinas sources products from Filipino local producers, small businesses, cooperatives, and artisan communities.

Can I choose products by province?

Yes. Customers can shop by region and province through the HabiPinas website using the Shop by Region page.

Are the products handmade?

Some products may be handmade or small-batch produced depending on the supplier. Product details will specify the source and production information.

Can I buy through Shopee?

Yes. Each product page includes a Buy on Shopee button for convenient checkout through our Shopee Official Store.

What payment methods do you accept?

HabiPinas accepts GCash, bank transfer, ShopeePay, and Shopee-supported payment methods, including Cash on Delivery through Shopee where available.

How long does delivery take?

Metro Manila orders may take 1–3 days. Luzon provincial orders may take 3–5 days. Visayas orders may take 5–7 days, and Mindanao orders may take 5–8 days.

How do I care for woven products?

Most woven products should be spot cleaned only and stored in a dry place. Avoid machine washing, harsh detergents, and excessive moisture.

Are color and pattern variations normal?

Yes. Since woven products may be handmade or small-batch produced, slight variations in color, pattern, and texture may occur. These are not considered defects.

What if my item arrives damaged?

Customers may report damaged items within 3 days of receiving the order by sending photos and order details through our Contact page or Messenger. The team will review and assist with replacement, return, or exchange.

Do you accept returns or exchanges?

Returns and exchanges are accepted only for wrong items, damaged items upon delivery, major product defects, or missing items. Items must be unused and in original packaging. See our Return & Exchange Policy for full details.
